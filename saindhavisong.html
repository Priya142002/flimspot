SAINDHAVI HITS


Saindhavi (born 3 January 1989) is an Indian carnatic vocalist and playback singer. She has been performing since the age of 12.Saindhavi married her schoolmate, composer G. V. Prakash Kumar, on 27 June 2013 in Chennai, India. They were in a relationship for 12 years before their wedding.The couple have a daughter.They announced their separation on 13 May 2024 after 11 years of marriage.
