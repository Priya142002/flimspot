Chutney Sambar Movie


Chutney Sambar is a 2024 Indian Tamil-language comedy drama television series directed by Radha Mohan for Disney+ Hotstar.[1] Produced by Ishari K. Ganesh under the banner of Vels Film International, the series stars Yogi Babu, Vani Bhojan and Chandran[2] in the lead role along with Nithin Sathya, Elango Kumaravel, Krish Hassan and Myna Nandhini.[3] It premiered on Disney+ Hotstar from 26 July 2024 and consisted of six episodes
